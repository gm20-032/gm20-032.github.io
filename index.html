Our Contribution & Technologies Used

After going through the onboarding process with the existing menu rater app, we made the following app improvements.

Dockerizing the App

Refactoring from Flask to Django

Replacing multithreading with Celery






Our first major milestone was dockerizing the front-end and back-end of the application. Dockerization allows us to be able to pack and ship the application easily as a container, making it much easier for our team members to get our environments synced up and working. Dockerization will also make deployment much simpler for other developers in the future.

Django and Flask are both web frameworks used to deploy web applications. We refactored the app to use Django instead of Flask. The switch was a little more difficult than initially expected, but we worked through it as a team.

Celery is like a task manager or queuing system that executes multiple concurrent processes. It is great for long runnings tasks like generating and sending a report. Another advantage of Celery over multithreading is its scalability.
